Get to Networking!

I didn’t spend all of my time going out to events and finding friends that could do things for me. Your network can be a combination of friends that have friends, folks you meet at the gym, work, or even the internet!

Connect with people and find out what they know. There are many online communities for your niche interests as well. Join slack groups and exchange referrals.

Many in my network are co-workers and friends. People I’ve met with similar interests. It happens in a magnetic way and then boom! Your network is expanding. Keep making friends!
